Your Safety, Our Priority: A Smart System for Tourists

A Robust Digital Ecosystem for Tourist Safety

TourSecure is a Smart Tourist Safety Monitoring & Incident Response System is designed to create a secure, technology-driven environment for visitors in high-risk and remote areas. By leveraging a combination of AI, Blockchain, and Geo-Fencing, we provide real-time monitoring and rapid response capabilities to ensure a safe travel experience.

The system comprises a secure digital ID platform, a user-friendly mobile application for tourists, and a real-time dashboard for authorities. Our solution aims to bridge the gap between traditional policing methods and the dynamic needs of modern tourism, ensuring peace of mind for both travelers and law enforcement.

Key Features

Digital Tourist ID

A secure, blockchain-based system that issues unique digital IDs to tourists. These IDs contain verified KYC, itinerary, and emergency contacts, and are valid for the duration of the visit.

Geo-Fencing Alerts

Receive automatic alerts when you enter a high-risk or restricted zone. This feature provides an extra layer of safety, guiding you through unfamiliar territories.

Panic Button

In case of an emergency, a single tap on the panic button shares your live location with the nearest police unit and your emergency contacts, ensuring a swift response.

AI Anomaly Detection

Our AI models detect unusual behavior such as sudden location drop-offs or prolonged inactivity. This helps authorities proactively identify and respond to potential distress situations.

Authority Dashboard

A real-time dashboard provides authorities with visual heat maps of tourist clusters, access to digital IDs, and automated E-FIR generation for missing person cases.
